## üé® Brand Consistency Tips

1. **Choose ONE photography style** and stick with it:
   - Option A: Minimalist white backgrounds
   - Option B: Lifestyle/model shots
   - Option C: Flatlay product photography

2. **Similar Color Tones:**
   - All warm (beige, cream, soft pink)
   - OR all cool (blue, gray, white)
   - Don't mix both

3. **Consistent Models** (if using people):
   - Similar age range
   - Similar styling
   - Diverse but cohesive
